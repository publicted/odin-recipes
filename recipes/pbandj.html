Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a classic American sandwich. Simple and yet delicious.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter - creamy or crunchy
  • white bread - two slices
  • jelly - flavor of your choice

Steps

  1. optional - toast the bread to desired doneness
  2. spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and the jelly on the inside
  5. optional - slice the sandwith in half
